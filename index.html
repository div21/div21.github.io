About Our Vigyanshala

Our learning center is an initiative designed for all children having dreams and desires to excel in their areas of interest and open a horizon of opportunities for them. We aim to design a process where children enjoy learning, a place which fuels their inquisitiveness and encourages to have out of the box thinking. We firmly believe that learning with nature and the surroundings will enhance and help them explore the self and beyond.
We adopt "Doing by self" as a means to learn and explore.
